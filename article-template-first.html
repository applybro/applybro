About This Article

The SSC CGL 2026 recruitment notification has been officially released by the Staff Selection Commission. This is one of the biggest government job opportunities for graduate-level candidates across India.

A total of 7,500+ vacancies are available for various Group B and Group C posts in different ministries, departments, and organizations of the Government of India.

Interested candidates can apply online through the official SSC website. The application process is completely online. Make sure to read the full notification before applying and keep all documents ready.

Eligibility Criteria
  • Candidate must be a citizen of India.
  • Must have a Bachelor's Degree from a recognized university.
  • Age should be between 18 to 32 years as per the official notification.
  • Other eligibility details are available in the official notification PDF.

Important Instructions
  • Read the official notification carefully before applying.
  • Use an active mobile number and email ID for all communication.
  • Upload clear and correct documents only. Blurry documents may reject your form.
  • Do not wait for the last date. Server may be slow due to heavy traffic.
  • Keep a printed copy of the submitted application form safe.
