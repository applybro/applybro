About This Article

CTET 2026 July Exam ka notification CBSE ne officially release kar diya hai. Yeh Central Government Schools (KVS, NVS) mein teacher banne ke liye sabse important exam hai.

CTET mein do papers hote hain — Paper I Class 1-5 ke teachers ke liye, aur Paper II Class 6-8 ke teachers ke liye. Aapke qualification ke hisaab se apply karein.

Exam Computer Based Test (CBT) mode mein hoga. Poora India mein exam centers hain. Notification carefully padhein aur form bharne se pehle eligibility check karein.

Eligibility Criteria
  • Candidate must be citizen of India.
  • Paper I: Senior Secondary (12th) with 50% marks + D.El.Ed / B.El.Ed.
  • Paper II: Graduation + B.Ed / B.El.Ed / D.El.Ed (with subject combination).
  • No Age Limit — koi bhi age ka candidate apply kar sakta hai.
