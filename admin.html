SEO Guide - Google Mein Rank Karne Ke Liye

Step-by-step instructions to get your website on Google

1

Google Search Console Mein Add Karein

search.google.com/search-console pe jayein

"Start now" pe click karein aur Google account se sign in karein

Domain option chunein aur applybro.in enter karein

Google ek TXT record dega - usse copy karein

Apne domain provider (GoDaddy/Namecheap) ke DNS mein TXT record add karein

"Verify" pe click karein

2

Sitemap.xml Banayein

Root folder mein sitemap.xml file banayein:

<?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8"?>
<urlset xmlns="http://www.sitemaps.org/schemas/sitemap/0.9">
  <url>
    <loc>https://www.applybro.in/</loc>
    <lastmod>2026-06-20</lastmod>
    <changefreq>daily</changefreq>
    <priority>1.0</priority>
  </url>
  <url>
    <loc>https://www.applybro.in/sarkari-jobs</loc>
    <lastmod>2026-06-20</lastmod>
    <changefreq>daily</changefreq>
    <priority>0.9</priority>
  </url>
</urlset>
3

Robots.txt Banayein

Root folder mein robots.txt file banayein:

User-agent: *
Allow: /
Disallow: /admin
Disallow: /private

Sitemap: https://www.applybro.in/sitemap.xml
4

Sitemap Google Ko Submit Karein

Google Search Console mein "Sitemaps" pe jayein

sitemap.xml enter karein

"Submit" pe click karein

5

URL Inspection & Indexing Request

GSC mein "URL Inspection" pe jayein

Homepage URL daalein: https://www.applybro.in/

"Request Indexing" pe click karein

Important SEO Tips

Har article mein unique title aur meta description lagayein
Content regularly update karein - daily best hai
Images mein alt text zaroor lagayein
Website speed fast rakhein - mobile friendly hona chahiye
Social media pe share karein - backlinks banayein
Keyword research karein - "Sarkari Job 2026" jaise
